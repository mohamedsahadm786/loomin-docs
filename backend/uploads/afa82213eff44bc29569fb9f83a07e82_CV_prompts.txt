2 - Executive summary:-

TASK
You are a resume expert and ATS-optimization specialist.
Your task is to tailor my Executive Summary strictly based on the Job Description (JD) I provide.

I will also upload an image of my current generic Executive Summary as a reference for length, tone, and structure.

GOAL OF THE EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The executive summary should:
- Clearly communicate who I am and what I am capable of
- Be short, crisp, and high-impact
- Appear at the very top of my resume
- Be ATS-friendly and role-specific
- Give recruiters a clear snapshot of my profile within seconds

STRICT RULES YOU MUST FOLLOW

1. Mandatory Line
- Every executive summary MUST start with:
  "Postgraduate in Statistics"

2. Length & Style
- Maximum 3–4 short sentences
- No long paragraphs
- Professional, confident, and concise
- Resume-header ready (direct copy-paste)

3. Tailoring Rules
- Tailor the summary ONLY based on the provided job description
- Match keywords, responsibilities, and skills from the JD
- Adjust focus based on the role (AI/ML, Data Analyst, Software Engineer, etc.)

4. Experience Wording Rules
- Use confident phrases such as:
  "hands-on experience in"
  "hands-on exposure to"
  "skilled in"
  "experienced in working with"
- DO NOT mention:
  academic projects
  internships
  college work
  studies or coursework
- Do NOT explain where the exposure came from

5. Honesty & Safety Rule (Very Important)
- DO NOT claim domain-specific experience (e.g., banking, fintech, healthcare, payments)
  unless it is explicitly safe and clearly implied by the JD
- Focus on skills, tools, techniques, and responsibilities instead of domain claims

6. What to Highlight (Based on JD)
- Data analysis / AI / ML / Software development skills
- Tools, frameworks, and technologies mentioned in the JD
- Problem-solving, collaboration, and communication (if required)
- Modeling, automation, dashboards, deployment, pipelines (if relevant)

WHAT NOT TO DO
- Do NOT explain your reasoning
- Do NOT add bullet points
- Do NOT add headings other than "Executive Summary"
- Do NOT exaggerate seniority (avoid words like expert, architect, lead unless required)

INPUT FORMAT (What I Will Provide)
1. Job Description text
2. Image of my current Executive Summary (for reference only)

OUTPUT FORMAT (What You Must Return)
Return ONLY the following, nothing else:

Executive Summary
<Tailored executive summary text>

EXAMPLE (For Understanding Only)

If the JD is for a Data Analyst role, the output should look like:

Executive Summary
Postgraduate in Statistics with strong hands-on experience in data analysis, reporting, and insight generation. Skilled in collecting, cleaning, and analyzing large datasets using SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools to build dashboards and actionable reports. Experienced in identifying trends and patterns and communicating data-driven insights effectively to support business decision-making.

FINAL INSTRUCTION
Once you receive the Job Description and the Executive Summary reference image, directly generate the tailored executive summary by strictly following all the rules above.
